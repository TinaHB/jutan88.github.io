There are Three Counselling Opportunities in the Treatment Pathway for Patients with stage III unresectable non-small cell lung cancer.

Initial discussion with patient to outline full treatment pathway of chemoradiation and immunotherapy.

Opportunity to counsel patients on immunotherapy prior to the initiation of immunotherapy.

During follow-up visits, reinforce the benefit of treatment continuation with immunotherapy to ensure patients complete the treatment course.

This website was developed by a scientific planning committee of Canadian lung cancer experts including medical oncologists, radiation oncologists, an oncology pharmacist and an oncology nurse, for educational purposes and should be used with patients to help them make the most informed treatment decision.
