To add in an appointment for a single day

  • Click on the desired date on the calendar
  • A window should pop-up asking you to enter Event Content
  • Enter the appointment details and click “OK” to save

To add in recurring appointments that occurs across several days:

  • Click on the start date and hold the left-click button
  • While holding the button, drag the cursor across the calendar to the end date
  • Once you reach the end date, release the left-click button and a window should pop-up asking you to enter Event Content
  • Enter the appointment details and click “OK” to save
